
Schema for property_data.csv:
- price (float): Final sale price of the property.
- bedrooms (int): Number of bedrooms.
- bathrooms (int): Number of bathrooms.
- square_feet (float): Total internal area in square feet.
- epc_rating (str): EPC rating (A-G).
- heating_type (str): Heating system type (e.g. Gas, Electric, Heat pump).
